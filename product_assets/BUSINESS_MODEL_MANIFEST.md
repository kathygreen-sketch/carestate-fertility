# CareState Fertility: Business Model Manifest (The Orchestration Layer)

## 1. Core Value Proposition: The "Independent Orchestrator"
CareState Fertility is not a clinic, a pharmacy, or a lender. It is the **Orchestration Layer** (the "Missing Middle") that sits between patients and the fragmented fertility industry.

*   **For Patients:** We provide a "Sanctuary" of clarity, solving **Instruction Amnesia** and **Injection Panic** through the "Now Card" (24-hour focus) and "9 PM SOS" triage.
*   **For Clinics:** We provide **Administrative Relief**, automating the staffing-heavy loop-closing and coordination tasks that currently burden nursing teams.

## 3. Revenue Engine (The Hybrid Model)
CareState employs a high-margin, asset-light revenue model designed for a \$100M+ exit.

### A. B2B SaaS (The "Epic" Portal)
*   **Target:** Clinics and Specialty Pharmacies.
*   **Fee:** ~$1,500/month per location.
*   **Value:** Firing the manual "Accidental Supervisor" (the nurse) in favor of system-led automation.

### B. B2C Pro Subscriptions (The "Sanctuary")
*   **Model:** Freemium "Canva" style.
*   **Pro Tier:** ~$29/cycle.
*   **Trigger:** Conversion occurs at "Moments of Risk" (e.g., first injection night, post-sedation handoff).

### C. Marketplace Referral Yield (The "Expedia" Layer)
*   **Blended Yield:** ~$3,000 per patient cycle.
*   **Components:** 
    *   Clinical Referral: $2,250
    *   Financing (Loan %): $600
    *   Pharma Rebates: $400
    *   *Total adjusted for conversion/volume.*
