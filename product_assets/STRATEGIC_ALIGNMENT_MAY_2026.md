## 3. Critical Product Requirements (The "Fortress Build")
Product Dept must prioritize the following modules in the next sprint:

### A. The "Now Card" (24-Hour Clinical Focus)
*   **Objective:** Eliminate instruction amnesia and decision fatigue.
*   **Logic:** Suppress all long-term data; show only the next 24 hours of required action (dosage, timing, verification).

### B. "In-Clinic Capture" (Voice/OCR Engine)
*   **Objective:** Digitization of physician instructions in real-time.
*   **Spec:** Hands-free capture at the doctor’s office to populate the Now Card automatically.

### C. "Retrieval Mode" (Support Handoff)
*   **Objective:** The "Trojan Horse" for B2B adoption.
*   **Logic:** A read-only supervisor view for support persons post-sedation, ensuring clinical precision when the patient is incapacitated.

### D. "9 PM SOS" Crisis Bridge
*   **Objective:** Pre-programmed clinical triage for common injection errors (air bubbles, missed doses, fridge failure).
*   **Redundancy:** Integrated "Bridge Rx" logic for local pharmacy pickups.
